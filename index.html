DUSTWARD

Single-player 3D sandbox RPG prototype.

  • Move: WASD or Arrow keys
  • Look: Mouse (click game to lock pointer) or Arrow Left/Right
  • Attack: Space
  • Interact: E
  • Use potion: Q
  • Toggle map: M, Fullscreen: F
Goal: accept quests from villagers, gather crystals and wood, hunt slimes, level up.
